About PingPal

We're on a mission to make uptime monitoring simple, reliable, and accessible for developers and teams worldwide.

Our Mission

At PingPal, we believe that monitoring your websites and APIs shouldn't be complicated or expensive. We're dedicated to providing developers and businesses with the tools they need to ensure their services stay online and perform optimally.


Founded in 2023 by a team of experienced developers who were frustrated with existing monitoring solutions, PingPal was born from the need for a simple, intuitive, and powerful monitoring platform that doesn't break the bank.


Today, we serve thousands of customers worldwide, from solo developers to enterprise teams, helping them maintain 99.9% uptime and respond to incidents faster than ever before.

Our Values

Customer First

Everything we build starts with our customers' needs. We listen, learn, and iterate based on real feedback from real users.

Keep It Simple

Complexity is the enemy of reliability. We strive to make our products intuitive and easy to use without sacrificing power.

Reliability First

We can't monitor your uptime if we're not up ourselves. Our infrastructure is built for 99.99% uptime with global redundancy.
